COMMENTS AND SUGGESTIONS


The Wind Turbine Airfoil Catalogue is intended to be a dynamic database. Therefore, your suggestions and comments are very welcome in order to improve the services this site provides. In particular, if you notice any error (wrong references or links, missing informations, etc...), please inform us.

As examples, here are some possible suggestions that you can come up with:

  • Compute a particular airfoil not considered yet
  • Perform further simulations on an airfoil already present in the catalogue (different Reynolds number, different angles of attack, etc...)
  • Add other functionalities and services to this website

You are welcome to contact us here.
